Our Mission

At Pippa of London, we believe everyone deserves to find their perfect match. Traditional shade matching often fails people with diverse skin tones, leading to disappointment, returns, and waste.

We're changing that with AI technology that truly sees and celebrates every skin tone. Our advanced color matching system uses the inclusive Monk Skin Tone Scale and professional-grade color science to deliver accurate results for everyone.
