Quick Answers

Find instant answers to common questions

How do I get started?

Simply click "Try Now" to create a free account. Then take or upload a photo, and our AI will analyze your skin tone in seconds.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All image processing happens in your browser, and we never store your photos unless you explicitly save results to your account.

What if the match isn't perfect?

Our AI improves continuously. If a match isn't quite right, use the feedback feature to help us learn and provide better recommendations.

Can I use this on mobile?

Yes! Our platform works on any device with a web browser - no app download required. Just visit our site on your phone or tablet.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we're focused on the UK market, but we're planning to expand internationally soon. Join our newsletter for updates!

How can I partner with Pippa?

We offer SDK integration and white-label solutions for beauty brands. Contact us through the form above to discuss partnership opportunities.
