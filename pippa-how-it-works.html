The Technology Behind the Magic

Cutting-edge AI meets inclusive beauty science

🌈

Monk Skin Tone Scale

We use Google's inclusive 10-point skin tone scale, designed to accurately represent all skin tones. This ensures our technology works beautifully for everyone, regardless of ethnicity or skin color.

🎯

Delta E Color Matching

Our professional-grade color matching uses the Delta E algorithm - the same technology used in high-end color calibration - to ensure matches are accurate to within 3 units of perceptible difference.

Undertone Detection

Advanced spectral analysis identifies whether you have cool, warm, or neutral undertones by analyzing the subtle color variations in your skin that are invisible to the naked eye.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our color matching technology

How accurate is the color matching? ⌄

Our technology achieves professional-grade accuracy with a Delta E value of less than 3, which means the color difference is barely perceptible to the human eye. We've tested across 50+ diverse skin tones with consistently accurate results.

What lighting conditions work best? ⌄

Natural daylight provides the best results. Take your photo near a window with indirect sunlight, avoiding harsh shadows or direct sun. Our AI can compensate for various lighting conditions, but natural light ensures optimal accuracy.

Is my photo data secure? ⌄

Absolutely. All image processing happens directly in your browser - your photos never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to save results to your account. We're committed to privacy-first design and never store or share your personal images.

Does it work for all skin tones? ⌄

Yes! We use the Monk Skin Tone Scale specifically designed for inclusive representation. Our AI is trained on diverse datasets representing all skin tones, from the fairest to the deepest, ensuring accurate matches for everyone.

Can I save and share my results? ⌄

Yes, when you create a free account, you can save your color analysis results, build a favorites list, and even share your matches with friends. You'll also get personalized recommendations based on your saved preferences.
