1. Introduction

Welcome to Pippa of London ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered color matching service.

Our Privacy Promise

Your photos are processed locally in your browser by default. We don't store or transmit your images unless you explicitly choose to save results to your account.

By using our service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our service.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password.
  • Profile Information: Optional information such as age range, skin concerns, and beauty preferences.
  • Contact Information: When you contact us, we collect your name, email, and message content.
  • Analysis Results: If you choose to save results, we store your color analysis data and product preferences.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on site.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser settings, time zone.

Image Data

When you use our color matching service:

  • Images are processed locally in your browser
  • We extract only color data points, not identifiable features
  • Original images are not transmitted or stored unless you explicitly save results
  • Saved results include only anonymized color data and product matches

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Our Service: Process color analysis and provide shade recommendations
  • Improve Accuracy: Enhance our AI models using anonymized, aggregated data
  • Personalize Experience: Remember your preferences and provide tailored recommendations
  • Communicate: Send service updates, respond to inquiries, provide customer support
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

We Never

Sell your personal information to third parties or use your photos for any purpose other than color analysis.

4. AI and Image Processing

Local Processing

Our AI technology is designed with privacy in mind:

  • Face detection and color analysis happen entirely in your browser
  • No images are uploaded to our servers during analysis
  • We use client-side machine learning models
  • Only numerical color values are extracted, not facial features

Optional Cloud Features

If you choose to use advanced features:

  • Saved results are encrypted and associated with your account
  • Historical analysis data helps improve recommendations
  • You can delete your saved data at any time

Model Training

To improve our service:

  • We use only anonymized, aggregated color data
  • No individual photos are used for training
  • Feedback data is anonymized before processing
  • We follow industry best practices for AI ethics

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share information in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We work with trusted partners who help us operate our service:

  • Cloud hosting providers (Microsoft Azure)
  • Analytics services (anonymized data only)
  • Customer support tools
  • Payment processors (if applicable)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, user information may be transferred with appropriate privacy protections.

Aggregated Information

We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics about our service usage that cannot identify individual users.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard TLS
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident Response: Procedures to detect and respond to data breaches
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for our team

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Portability

You can request a copy of your personal data in a portable format.

Correction

You can update or correct your information through your account settings or by contacting us.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Restriction

You can ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.

Objection

You can object to certain types of processing, such as marketing communications.

Withdrawal of Consent

Where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@pippaoflondon.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use our service
  • Improve our website performance

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand usage patterns (anonymized)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that information.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification for material changes

Your continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
